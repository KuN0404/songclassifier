music history

Music history dates back to ancient times when early humans used rhythm and sound to communicate. Over the centuries, different cultures developed distinct musical styles, with instruments like drums and stringed instruments emerging. The evolution of music continued with the rise of classical compositions in the 17th and 18th centuries, followed by the birth of modern genres like jazz, rock, and electronic music.

In the 20th century, popular music exploded with the advent of radio, records, and the internet. Genres like rock, pop, and hip-hop became mainstream, while electronic dance music (EDM) rose to global prominence in the late 1990s and early 2000s. Today, music is a universal language, continuously evolving with technological advances and cultural influences.
